About Me

Hello, my name is Elaine Lagon and I love all things code as my passion is Web Development. I think it has always been that ever since my senior year of high school. I get so excited when there are new frameworks or new layouts to try because I love learning new things.

My framework of choice for now is Bootstrap, but when not in use, I dive deep into Responsive Design. I find that I have a much higher appreciation of being able to control my own styling using media queries.

Code editor: Visual Studio Code.

I graduated Summa Cum Laude, and have a Bachelors of Science Degree in Multimedia Design and Web Development. Currently, I am in school to gain more skills for the workforce.

When I am not working on websites, I like to practice on the drums in my spare time, or play/write songs with my band A1terMyth (website coming next year). Of course there's other priorities, such as spending time with family and friends.
